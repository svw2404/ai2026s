作業 05

Before doing this homework, I only had a basic idea of image generation tools, and I was more familiar with StableDiffusion-WebUI. This assignment helped me understand ComfyUI in a much more practical way. At first, the node-based interface looked confusing, but after working with it I realized that it clearly shows how Checkpoint, LoRA, positive prompt, negative prompt, KSampler, VAE Decode, and Save Image are connected. I also learned that image generation is not only about writing prompts, but also about choosing the correct model, adjusting parameters, and managing GPU limitations. During the process, I had issues with VRAM and the NVIDIA driver, so I understood why resolution, steps, batch size, and workflow complexity matter. In the end, successfully generating the Frieren and Himmel images made me feel more confident using ComfyUI and understanding the Stable Diffusion workflow.
